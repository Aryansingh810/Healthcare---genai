About MediScribe AI

Purpose

MediScribe AI is a specialized Generative AI application for healthcare professionals. It converts raw patient inputs—symptoms, medical reports, and clinical observations—into high-quality, professional clinical summaries suitable for documentation and care coordination.

Problems in Manual Healthcare Documentation

Healthcare providers spend significant time on administrative tasks. Manual documentation leads to:

  • Inconsistent formatting across notes and reports
  • Delays in patient record updates
  • Higher risk of human error and omissions
  • Less time available for direct patient care

How Generative AI Helps Doctors

By leveraging large language models and vector databases, MediScribe AI standardizes clinical summaries, maintains a professional medical tone, and structures output with clear headings such as Chief Complaints, Clinical Findings, Assessment, and Recommendations. Doctors can focus on patient care while the system handles documentation support.

Accuracy, Consistency & Time Savings

Accuracy is paramount in healthcare. MediScribe AI uses formal medical terminology, avoids unsupported assumptions, and produces consistent formatting. This reduces documentation time and supports better care continuity across providers.

Tech Stack

MediScribe AI is built with Python and Flask on the backend. It uses a Vector Database (ChromaDB) for storing and retrieving patient data, and the Google Gemini API for generating clinical summaries. The frontend uses HTML and Tailwind CSS for a clean, minimal interface.
